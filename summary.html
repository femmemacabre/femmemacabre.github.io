Act 1
In the wake of the Antichrist’s passing, the city of Satania stands as a ruin of shadows, where faith, survival, and power are at play. Amongst its few survivors are Monika Iris, a half-demon forged in mystery; Bowie, clinging to grief and memory; and Aether Luminous, a fallen seraphim torn between rebellion and redemption. But their refuge fractures when the forbidden hour unveils truths better left forgotten. Forces both divine and profane circle overhead—while in the depths below, new powers begin to fashion weapons meant to unmake all life. What begins as survival in a broken city soon reveals itself as part of a cruder reality, one that threatens to consume far more than the singular cityscape.
