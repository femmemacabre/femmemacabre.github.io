Main Characters (Act I - His Machiavellian Grace)
Monika Iris

Demon forged by the ink machine and raised on Earth. One of the few survivors of The Great Indoctrination.
Caring and loving, yet defensive and protective.
Lives with her living companion, Bowie, in the Kaisarion. When everything seems bleak, the fate of all appears to be in her hands.
Bowie

Lone angel whose family died in The Great Indoctrination faces the Neon Genesis with her living companion, Monika.
Caring yet reserved and somewhat secluded.
Grief-striken, wounded and bitter, she hides her true self from her best friend. In the end it might be too much to handle.
Aether Marchionne Luminous

Eldest son of the most powerful family in Heaven.
Short tempered, a bit paranoid and tends to avoid opening up due to how he was raised. After being banished from the Council of Angels and disowned by his father, he takes bringing back the city of Dei into his own hands.
Tobias Svensson

One of the Gangsters and the most outward and upbeat. Being pulled from his dimension, he and his group start heists in this new world but get caught up in the mission to end all.
Complete scaredy-cat but sweet and clingy.
Is best friends with Kyre.
Kyre Rochefort

The self-proclaimed leader of the Gangsters. Very reserved and secluded. Being pulled from his dimension, he kickstarts new heists in this new world and among all the chaos he meets someone very special. Very abrupt  but selective and intelligent.
Rayne Anghel

The final member of the Gansters. Much like Kyre, she is very reserved but is much more open with those she likes. Caring and carefree.
Her radioactive hair has given her the ability to use radioactivity at will and will not hesitate to take out anyone in her path.
Tobias only remains in the Gangsters because Rayne voted in his favor.
Anna Gatto

A third angel, third human and third cat. This unique hybrid is very shy and timid and has a tough time utilizing comprehension skills in everyday situations.
Very worrysome and methodical. Being pulled from her dimension she finds herself in this strange new world and it will not be easy on her.
Mikael Avrgrund

Once a pope for the people of Dei and now an alchemist working on the side of voodoo Gods against the angel race. Has lost all and any emotion he may have once had.
Nothing's been the same since the accident.
Cation Kaiko

Subordinate to Sr. Mikael.
Was almost executed by his higher-ups but was allowed to live if he assisted Mikael in his discoveries.
Lives in pure fright for his own life.
Very outward if he thinks it's appropriate
Yose Diaz

An outcase of his own kind hellbent on revenge against all. His plan is an ultimatum to destroy everything and anything. Betraying anyone who helps him to get what he wants.
Cartoonishly evil and very narcissistic.
